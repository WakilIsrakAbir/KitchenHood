Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I clean my kitchen hood?
For residential use, we recommend deep cleaning every 6-8 months. For heavy cooking (frying, grilling), every 3-4 months. Commercial kitchens should be cleaned monthly to comply with fire safety regulations.
Do you provide warranty on repairs?
Yes! All our repair services come with a comprehensive 6-month warranty. If the same issue recurs within the warranty period, we fix it completely free of charge. New installations carry a 1-year warranty.
Which brands do you service?
We service all major kitchen hood brands including Elica, Faber, Kutchina, Bosch, Siemens, Miyako, Walton, Prestige, and more. Our technicians are factory trained on Italian and German models.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Cash on Delivery, bKash, Nagad, Rocket, and bank transfer. For products ordered online, payment is collected upon delivery. Service charges are paid after completion and your satisfaction.
