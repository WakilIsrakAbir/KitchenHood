Our Story

Setting the Standard in
Appliance Care

KitchenHood Pro was founded in 2016 with a clear mission: to provide transparent, reliable, and expert technical support for kitchen ventilation systems across Bangladesh. Today, with over 15,000 successful service completions and a 98% customer satisfaction rate, we are recognized as the most trusted name in kitchen hood services in Dhaka.

What Drives Us

Our Core Principles

Authentic Parts Only

We strictly prohibit aftermarket components. Every part we install is sourced directly from authorized distributors, with full traceability and warranty coverage.

Punctual Scheduling

Our technicians arrive precisely within the committed service window. We respect your time and maintain a 95% on-time arrival rate across all bookings.

Transparent Pricing

No hidden charges, no surprise fees. We provide detailed cost breakdowns before starting any work. You only pay for what was agreed upon upfront.

Zero Mess Guarantee

We use professional drop cloths, carry cleaning supplies, and leave your kitchen spotless after every service. If we mess it, we clean it — guaranteed.
