Critical Safety Information

Why Regular Maintenance is Non-Negotiable

Ignoring your kitchen hood leads to catastrophic safety hazards, expensive breakdowns, and unhealthy air quality. Here's what happens when maintenance is skipped.

Kitchen Fire Risk

Accumulated grease inside the hood and ducting is extremely flammable. A single cooking spark can ignite a devastating kitchen fire, causing thousands in damage and putting lives at risk. Regular degreasing eliminates this threat entirely.

Premature Motor Death

Clogged filters force the extraction motor to work at 200% capacity. This causes overheating, coil burnout, and bearing failure — resulting in a ৳5,000-15,000 replacement cost that could have been prevented with a simple ৳1,500 cleaning.

Toxic Air Exposure

A blocked hood fails to extract smoke, carbon monoxide, and cooking fumes. Your family breathes in harmful particulates daily, leading to respiratory problems, persistent headaches, and greasy walls and ceilings throughout your home.
